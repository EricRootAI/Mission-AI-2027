# Example 3 – Standard Operating Procedure

## Original Business Scenario

A Front Office Manager needs to create a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for front desk cash handling to ensure consistency, accountability, and compliance across all shifts.

## AI Output

# Standard Operating Procedure
## Front Desk Cash Handling

### Purpose

To establish standardized procedures for handling cash transactions accurately, securely, and professionally.

### Scope

This procedure applies to all Front Desk employees responsible for processing cash payments.

### Responsibilities

- Count starting cash drawer before each shift.
- Verify all cash transactions.
- Issue receipts for every payment.
- Secure cash drawer when unattended.
- Report discrepancies immediately.

### Procedure

1. Verify beginning cash balance.
2. Process cash transactions accurately.
3. Count change carefully before returning it to the guest.
4. Record all transactions in the property management system.
5. Balance the cash drawer at the end of the shift.
6. Complete reconciliation documentation.
7. Submit deposits according to company policy.

### Safety Considerations

- Never leave a cash drawer unlocked.
- Avoid counting cash in view of guests.
- Report suspicious activity immediately.

### Escalation Process

Any cash variance exceeding company policy should be reported immediately to the Manager on Duty.

## Business Value

Provides standardized operating procedures while significantly reducing documentation time for managers.

# Example 4 – Coaching Conversation

## Original Business Scenario

A supervisor needs to coach an employee regarding repeated tardiness while maintaining a respectful and supportive leadership approach.

## AI Output

**Manager:**

Thanks for meeting with me today. I wanted to discuss your attendance because I've noticed you've arrived late three times over the past two weeks.

**Employee:**

I understand. I've been having transportation issues recently.

**Manager:**

I appreciate your honesty. I know unexpected situations happen, but consistent attendance is important because it impacts the entire team and our guests.

**Employee:**

I understand and I'm working on fixing the issue.

**Manager:**

Thank you. Let's create a plan together. If you anticipate being late, please communicate with us as early as possible. We'll review your attendance over the next 30 days and check in again.

**Employee:**

That sounds fair. Thank you.

## Business Value

Creates professional coaching conversations that encourage accountability while maintaining employee engagement.

# Human Review and Responsible AI Use

Every AI-generated output included in this portfolio is intended to serve as a professional first draft.

Before implementation, all AI-generated content should be reviewed by an appropriate leader to ensure:

- Accuracy
- Compliance with organizational policies
- Protection of confidential information
- Appropriate tone and professionalism
- Alignment with legal, ethical, and operational standards

Generative AI is most effective when used as a collaborative productivity tool that enhances human expertise, supports informed decision-making, and allows hospitality professionals to spend more time delivering exceptional guest experiences.
