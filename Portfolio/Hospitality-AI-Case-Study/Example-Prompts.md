# Hospitality AI Example Prompt Library

This document demonstrates practical examples of prompt engineering designed to improve operational efficiency, employee productivity, and guest satisfaction within hospitality organizations.

Each prompt represents a real-world business scenario where generative AI can assist leaders while maintaining appropriate human oversight.

## Prompt 1 – Shift Handoff Summary

### Business Scenario

A hotel manager needs to summarize the outgoing shift for the incoming leadership team.

### Prompt

You are an experienced hospitality operations manager.

Using the notes below, create a concise professional shift handoff report.

Include:

• Guest concerns
• Maintenance issues
• Staffing updates
• VIP arrivals
• Outstanding tasks
• Follow-up priorities

Write in a clear, executive style.

### Business Value

Reduces time spent writing shift reports while improving communication between leadership teams.

## Prompt 9 – AI Scheduling Assistant

### Business Scenario

A department manager needs to create a fair weekly schedule while balancing business demand, employee availability, labor budgets, and overtime.

### Prompt

You are an experienced hospitality operations manager.

Using the employee availability, business forecast, labor budget, and staffing requirements provided below, create a weekly work schedule.

Requirements:

• Ensure all critical positions are staffed.
• Balance employee hours fairly whenever possible.
• Minimize overtime.
• Identify any staffing gaps or scheduling conflicts.
• Recommend adjustments if coverage cannot be achieved.
• Explain your scheduling decisions.

Present the final schedule in an easy-to-read table.

### Business Value

Assists managers in creating balanced schedules more efficiently while identifying potential staffing issues before the schedule is published.

## Prompt 10 – Employee Recognition Generator

### Business Scenario

A hospitality leader wants to recognize an employee for delivering exceptional guest service during a busy shift.

### Prompt

Write a sincere employee recognition message celebrating outstanding guest service.

The message should:

• Describe the employee's accomplishment.
• Highlight the positive impact on guests and the team.
• Reinforce the organization's service culture.
• Maintain a warm, professional, and encouraging tone.
• Be suitable for a recognition board, team meeting, or company newsletter.

Keep the message between 150–250 words.

### Business Value

Helps leaders consistently recognize outstanding performance, reinforce positive workplace culture, and improve employee engagement through meaningful appreciation.
