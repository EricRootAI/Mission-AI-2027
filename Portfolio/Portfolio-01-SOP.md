Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Coffee Shop Opening Procedure

Business: Sunrise Coffee Company
Department: Store Operations
Document Number: SOP-OPS-001
Effective Date: July 22, 2026
Version: 1.0

⸻

Purpose

The purpose of this Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is to establish a consistent, efficient, and safe process for opening Sunrise Coffee Company each business day. Following this procedure ensures operational readiness, exceptional customer service, food safety compliance, and a welcoming environment for guests.

⸻

Scope

This SOP applies to all Store Managers, Assistant Managers, Shift Supervisors, and Opening Team Members responsible for preparing the store before customers arrive.

⸻

Responsibilities

Store Manager

* Ensure opening procedures are followed consistently.
* Verify completion of opening checklist.
* Address operational issues before opening.
* Monitor compliance with company standards.

Shift Supervisor

* Lead the opening team.
* Delegate opening responsibilities.
* Confirm equipment functionality.
* Complete required documentation.

Team Members

* Complete assigned opening tasks.
* Follow food safety procedures.
* Maintain cleanliness and organization.
* Immediately report equipment or safety concerns.

⸻

Required Materials

* Store opening checklist
* Store keys/access credentials
* Cash drawer and safe access
* Point-of-sale (POS) system
* Coffee beans
* Milk and dairy alternatives
* Syrups and sauces
* Cups, lids, sleeves, and napkins
* Bakery and food inventory
* Cleaning supplies
* Sanitizing solution
* Temperature log
* Employee communication log

⸻

Step-by-Step Opening Procedure

1. Arrive and Secure Entry

* Arrive at least 45–60 minutes before opening.
* Inspect the exterior for safety concerns.
* Unlock entrance.
* Disarm security system.
* Turn on interior lights.
* Conduct a quick security walkthrough.

⸻

2. Perform Safety Inspection

Verify:

* No hazards on the floor.
* Emergency exits are clear.
* Fire extinguisher is accessible.
* Refrigerators are operating correctly.
* No water leaks.
* Electrical equipment appears safe.

Report any issues immediately.

⸻

3. Activate Store Equipment

Power on:

* Espresso machines
* Coffee brewers
* Grinders
* Ovens
* Warmers
* Ice machine (if required)
* POS terminals
* Digital menu displays
* Music system

Allow espresso machines sufficient warm-up time according to manufacturer recommendations.

⸻

4. Prepare Beverage Station

* Brew fresh coffee.
* Calibrate grinders if needed.
* Pull test espresso shots.
* Steam and purge espresso equipment.
* Prepare iced coffee or cold brew stations.
* Stock milk pitchers.
* Fill syrup containers.
* Restock tea products.

Taste-test brewed coffee to ensure quality.

⸻

5. Food Preparation

* Verify food temperatures.
* Rotate inventory using FIFO (First In, First Out).
* Prepare bakery display.
* Stock refrigerated grab-and-go items.
* Discard expired products.
* Record waste if required.

⸻

6. Stock Customer Service Areas

Refill:

* Cups
* Lids
* Sleeves
* Straws
* Napkins
* Sugar packets
* Sweeteners
* Stir sticks
* Condiments

Ensure displays are neat and fully stocked.

⸻

7. Cash Handling

* Count opening cash drawer.
* Verify starting cash amount.
* Document beginning balance.
* Load drawer into POS.
* Secure excess cash in safe.

Report discrepancies immediately.

⸻

8. Clean and Sanitize

Sanitize:

* Counters
* Espresso station
* Beverage preparation surfaces
* Dining tables
* Restrooms
* Door handles
* Condiment station

Empty trash if necessary.

⸻

9. Complete Operational Checks

Confirm:

* Wi-Fi operational
* Internet functioning
* POS connected
* Receipt printers operational
* Mobile ordering active
* Loyalty program functioning
* Credit card terminals operational

⸻

10. Final Readiness Check

Verify:

* Background music appropriate.
* Lighting adjusted.
* Store temperature comfortable.
* Exterior clean.
* Patio prepared (if applicable).
* Staff in proper uniform.
* Name tags visible.
* Opening checklist completed.

Unlock doors at scheduled opening time.

⸻

Quality Standards

The store must meet the following standards before opening:

* Coffee brewed within freshness standards.
* Espresso shots meet quality specifications.
* Food displays fully stocked.
* Customer areas spotless.
* Restrooms clean and stocked.
* Equipment fully operational.
* Employees professionally presented.
* No visible safety hazards.
* Inventory organized and rotated.

⸻

Safety Considerations

Employees must:

* Wash hands before food preparation.
* Wear required personal protective equipment when appropriate.
* Use caution around hot surfaces and steam.
* Lift heavy items using proper techniques.
* Immediately clean spills.
* Keep exits unobstructed.
* Follow food safety temperature guidelines.
* Report injuries immediately.

Never operate damaged equipment.

⸻

Troubleshooting

Espresso Machine Not Heating

* Verify power supply.
* Restart machine.
* Check water connection.
* Notify manager if issue persists.

Coffee Grinder Malfunction

* Inspect for jammed beans.
* Clean grinder.
* Reset equipment.
* Use backup grinder if available.

POS System Offline

* Restart terminal.
* Verify internet connection.
* Contact technical support.
* Initiate approved offline payment procedures if necessary.

Refrigerator Temperature Out of Range

* Record temperature.
* Notify manager immediately.
* Relocate temperature-sensitive products if required.
* Contact maintenance.

Cash Drawer Shortage

* Recount cash.
* Verify documentation.
* Notify supervisor before opening.
