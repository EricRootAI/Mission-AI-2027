Sales Strategy Consulting Report

Regional HVAC Company

Prepared by: Business Strategy Consultant
Purpose: Develop a structured sales strategy to increase revenue, improve lead conversion, and establish a scalable sales process for a regional HVAC company.

⸻

Executive Summary

The regional HVAC market is highly competitive, with success driven by customer trust, response time, service quality, and consistent lead generation. This strategy focuses on increasing qualified leads, improving close rates, strengthening customer retention, and building recurring revenue through maintenance agreements.

The plan combines digital marketing, referral programs, community engagement, structured sales processes, and data-driven performance management to support sustainable growth.

⸻

Sales Goals

Primary Objectives (12 Months)

* Increase annual revenue by 20%
* Increase qualified inbound leads by 30%
* Improve sales close rate from 35% to 45%
* Increase average job value by 15%
* Double HVAC maintenance agreement memberships
* Increase customer referral business by 25%
* Reduce customer acquisition cost (CAC) by 15%
