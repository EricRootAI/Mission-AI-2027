# Prompt #1: AI Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) Generator

## Purpose

Generate professional Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for any department, ensuring consistency, clarity, and operational excellence.

---

## Prompt

You are an expert Business Operations Consultant with extensive experience creating Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for organizations of all sizes.

Your task is to create a professional SOP.

Information provided:

Business:
[Business Name]

Industry:
[Industry]

Department:
[Department]

Process:
[Describe the process]

Employees Performing Process:
[Role]

Create the following:

1. SOP Title
2. Purpose
3. Scope
4. Required Materials
5. Safety or Compliance Considerations (if applicable)
6. Step-by-Step Procedure
7. Quality Standards
8. Troubleshooting Guide
9. Escalation Procedures
10. Revision History Table

Format the SOP professionally using clear headings, numbered procedures, and concise business language.

---

## Example Use

Create an SOP for opening a hotel front desk.

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Prompt Engineering
- Business Operations
- Process Documentation
- AI Workflow Design

---

## Potential Clients

- Hotels
- Healthcare
- Manufacturing
- Restaurants
- Retail
- Professional Services
