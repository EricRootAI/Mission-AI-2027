# AI Employee Onboarding Assistant v1

## Project Overview
An AI-powered workflow concept designed to improve employee onboarding by helping organizations create consistent training materials, welcome communications, and first-week success plans.

## Business Problem
Organizations often struggle with inconsistent onboarding experiences, unclear expectations, and time-consuming manual preparation.

## AI Solution
Using generative AI and structured prompting, this assistant helps create:

- Welcome emails
- First-week onboarding checklists
- 30-day employee success plans
- Training resources
- Manager communication templates

## Target Users
- Human Resources teams
- Operations leaders
- Training departments
- Small and medium businesses

## Skills Demonstrated
- Prompt Engineering
- Business Process Improvement
- AI Workflow Design
- Generative AI Applications
- Documentation
